Features of the Canvas Drawing Tool

Our Canvas Drawing Tool is a simple yet powerful online tool that allows you to unleash your creativity. Here are some of the features:

  • Draw Freely: Use the pencil tool to create freehand drawings with your mouse or touchpad.
  • Eraser Tool: Easily erase any part of your drawing with the eraser feature.
  • Color Picker: Choose from a wide range of colors to add vibrancy to your creations.
  • Clear Canvas: Start fresh anytime with the "Clear" button, which erases all your work.
  • Responsive: Works seamlessly across devices, so you can sketch on your computer, tablet, or phone.

How It Works

Getting started with our Canvas Drawing Tool is easy! Here's how to use it:

  1. Select Your Tool: Choose between the pencil or eraser tool to begin.
  2. Pick a Color: Use the color picker to select your preferred drawing color.
  3. Start Drawing: Click and drag to draw on the canvas area.
  4. Erase and Edit: If you make a mistake, simply switch to the eraser tool and remove unwanted lines.
  5. Clear the Canvas: Use the "Clear" button to start a new drawing or remove your existing work.
